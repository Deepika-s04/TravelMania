Greek Cuisine

Greek cuisine is a Mediterranean masterpiece, blending fresh ingredients with bold flavors. From olive oil-drenched salads to hearty grilled meats, every dish is a celebration of Greece’s sun-soaked landscapes. Savor meals in tavernas by the Aegean, where hospitality is as warm as the climate.

Classic Dishes

Enjoy Moussaka, a layered eggplant dish, or Souvlaki, grilled meat skewers. Try these at O Thanasis in Athens for authentic flavors.

Meze & Salads

Share Tzatziki, Hummus, or Greek Salad, bursting with feta and olives, at Psarades Taverna in Santorini.

Desserts & Drinks

Indulge in Baklava, a sweet pastry, or sip Ouzo, an anise-flavored spirit, at local tavernas.

Greece Weather

Greece enjoys a Mediterranean climate with mild winters and hot summers, perfect for island-hopping and sightseeing. Here’s what to expect:

Spring (Mar-May)

Mild and pleasant (15-25°C), with blooming wildflowers and fewer crowds.

Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and sunny (25-35°C), ideal for beaches and island adventures.

Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Warm and comfortable (15-28°C), great for exploring ruins and islands.

Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cool and rainy (5-15°C), with quieter sites and cozy tavernas.

Tip: Pack light clothing for summer, layers for winter, and comfortable shoes for exploring historic sites.
