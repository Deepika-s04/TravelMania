Indian Cuisine

Indian cuisine is a kaleidoscope of flavors, blending aromatic spices, fresh herbs, and regional diversity. From creamy curries to sizzling street food, every dish tells a story of tradition and innovation. Dine at bustling dhabas or elegant restaurants to experience India’s culinary soul.

Curries & Mains

Savor Butter Chicken, Rogan Josh, or Chole Bhature at Karim’s in Delhi for authentic tastes.

Street Food

Try Pani Puri, Vada Pav, or Dosa at local stalls or Sarafa Bazaar in Indore.

Sweets & Drinks

Indulge in Gulab Jamun or sip Masala Chai at traditional sweet shops like Haldiram’s.

India Weather

India’s climate varies widely, from tropical south to Himalayan north, offering diverse travel experiences. Here’s what to expect:

Summer (Mar-Jun)

Hot and dry (25-45°C), best for hill stations like Shimla.

Monsoon (Jul-Sep)

Humid with heavy rains (20-35°C), ideal for Kerala’s greenery.

Autumn (Oct-Nov)

Pleasant and clear (15-30°C), perfect for festivals and sightseeing.

Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cool and foggy (5-25°C), great for Delhi and Rajasthan.

Tip: Pack lightweight cotton for summer, rain gear for monsoon, and layers for winter, especially in the north.
